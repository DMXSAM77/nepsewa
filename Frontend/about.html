Who We Are

Nepal's Home Service Platform

NepSewa is a Butwal-based online platform launched in 2026 to connect everyday Nepali households with trusted, trained local professionals. Whether you need your pipes fixed, your home cleaned, your AC serviced, or a professional photographer for a family event — NepSewa brings them to your door.

We started small, with a team of 8 people and 40 registered professionals in the Lumbini Province. Today, we have grown to serve multiple cities across Nepal, with over 500 verified professionals and more than 10,000 completed bookings.

Our Story

Why We Built NepSewa

In Nepal, finding a reliable electrician, plumber, or home cleaner has always been a challenge. Most people depend on word of mouth — asking neighbours, calling relatives, or posting in Facebook groups hoping someone will respond. Even when you do find someone, there's no guarantee of pricing, quality, or punctuality.

By 2025, smartphone and internet usage across Nepal had grown significantly, especially among younger households. People were already booking food, clothes, and rides online — but home services were still stuck in the old way. That gap is what inspired us to build NepSewa.

We launched in January 2026 with a simple goal: make it as easy to book a plumber as it is to order food. No calls, no guessing, no overpaying. Just open the website, choose your service, pick a time, and a verified professional shows up.
